Harford County, Maryland

Location

Harford County is located in Northern Maryland about 36 miles from Baltimore City.

About

In 1773, Baltimore County was divided into Harford County. In honor of Henry Harford, the final Maryland proprietor, the county bears his name. Despite being the son of Frederick Calvert, 6th Lord Baltimore, he was not entitled to his father's title due to his illegitimate birth.

Weather

It's cold in the winter and hot in the summer.

Population

The population is 260,924 per the 2020 census.

Sports

The Baltimore Ravens and Oriels are our popular sports teams.

Things To Do

  • Visit Downtown Bel-air.
  • Visit Rock State Park.
  • Go to the Harford Mall.
